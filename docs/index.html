Superconducting-Toolkit (SCTK)

Superconducting-Toolkit is a package for computing the superconducting properties based on the density functional theory for superconductors (SCDFT).

For citing SCTK

We would appreciate if you cite the following article in your research with SCTK.

Benchmark of density functional theory for superconductors in elemental materials
Mitsuaki Kawamura, Yuma Hizume, and Taisuke Ozaki, Phys. Rev. B 101, 134511 (2020) ( arXiv:1911.06481).

Researches with SCTK

  • Theoretical prediction of superconductivity in monolayer CoO2
    Duc-Long Nguyen, Cheng-Rong Hsing and Ching-Ming Wei, Nanoscale 11, 17052 (2019).
  • Anisotropic superconducting gaps in YNi2B2C: A first-principles investigation
    M. Kawamura, R. Akashi, and S. Tsuneyuki, Phys. Reb. B 95, 054506 (2017) (arXiv:1610.07329).
